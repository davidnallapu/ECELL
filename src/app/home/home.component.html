About E-Cell SNU

The Entrepreneurship Cell of Shiv Nadar University is a student-run club, functioning under the aegis of Atal Incubation Centre, Niti Aayog, to cultivate this practice amongst the students of our prestigious institution.

Vision: E-cell Shiv Nadar University strives to provide a platform for students and faculty to create, innovate, incubate and realize their entrepreneurial dreams while encouraging to “think upside down” to venture beyond the normal.

Mission: To build an atmosphere to foster healthy discussions and ideas for students to take their entrepreneurial zeal to greater heights. We conduct sessions, interactive activities and workshops to nourish startup ideas and develop a strong network among the student base. We also provide an opportunity for incubation which handholds them until they are self-sustaining. We aim to create an approachable community where just an idea can be transformed into a working business model.

Workshops

Workshops to develop a better understanding of the entrepreneurial and corporate world have been conducted on pitching, finance, bootcamps, design thinking, brand building, ideation, marketing which engages students to interact and apply their stimuli.

Think Tank Series

Ecell SNU kickstarted it's E-Cell exclusive- THINK TANK series. It takes us through multiple modules ranging from all aspects of entrepreneurship where the members contribute to the pool through interactive sessions.

Venture Challenge

This is a one-of-its-kind B-Plan competition: with funds of ₹ 1,00,00,000 at stake. Just participation also grants access to a large network of experts and a complete entrepreneurial ecosystem at SNU.

Game changers

Interactive sessions to celebrate the spirit of World Entrepreneurs' Day with some of the best entrepreneurial minds in India. The event saw an audience of 300+ and was a pivotal event for E-cell, after which promotion of entrepreneurship accelerated
